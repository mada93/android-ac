Analog Compass

The intention of "Analog Compass" is to simulate the physical behavior of a real compass. So you won't find any digital features.

Features

  • choose a layout (2x)
  • retro style
  • smooth motion
  • calibration
  • set colors
  • set speed

If the compass shows a wrong direction, try

  • to move your device around in all directions to reset it. A more detailed instruction can be found here.
  • to calibrate the compass. Press Menu/"Calibrate" after the application has started and follow the instructions. The compass of my device (HTC Magic) for example points 9° to much to the east.
